     All articles are published in full open access. An article processing charge (APC) of 1 USD applies to papers accepted after peer review. This article processing charge is to cover the costs of peer review, copyediting, typesetting, long-term archiving, and journal management. In addition to USD, we also accept payment in euros (EUR), Swiss francs (CHF), British pound sterling (GBP), Japanese yen (JPY) or Canadian dollars (CAD). Invoices are emailed shortly after acceptance to the payment contact provided by the authors. Only official invoices issued by OASIS PUBLICATION are valid. We do not authorize any third party to collect the APCs. OASIS PUBLICATION is the sole service provider and cannot be held liable for actions by third parties. Local VAT or Sales Tax will be added if applicable.
